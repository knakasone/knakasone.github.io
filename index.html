About

I grew up in and am currently based in Southern California. I went to college at Northeastern University in Boston, MA and graduated with a degree in Computer Engineering in 2020. During college, I worked multiple coops (6 month full-time positions). I worked in Information Technology at Harvard Kennedy School and then Bluewave Solar, I determined that Information Technology was not a career that I wanted to pursue.


Because I have always been drawn to art and design, I realized that I should combine my passion for art with my programming experience by going in to front-end development. So far, I have learned HTML, CSS, and Javascript, and I am currently taking two web development classes for front-end and back-end while also doing personal projects on the side.
